I am the Chief Technology Officer at Twigkit, a Cambridge-based software company that provides tools for powering search-based applications. Before joining Twigkit, I did research in theoretical computer science (graphs, complexity and finite model theory) at the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory. I am @bjarkiholm on Twitter and you can find my CV on LinkedIn. You can also contact my directly using my Gmail address {firstName.lastName}.
